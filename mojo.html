MOJO CAFE


MOJO cafe was not only created, but now run by owners Adrian and Ingrid. Who ensures that their cafe not only has an excellent level of cleanliness, friendly service, and quality ingredients. But serves many dishes personally made and/or approved by Ingrid. The coffee is excellent, the muffins are huge and delicious, the salads are fresh and big enough for two! What more do you need for a coffee break or lunch - even a glass of wine is available!
