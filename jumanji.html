JUMANJI


ACTORS: Dwayne Johnson, Alex Wolff, Ser'Darius Blain, Jack Black, Madison Iseman, Karen Gillan, Morgan Turner, Nick Jonas, Colin Hanks, Mason Guccione, Rhys Darby, Bobby Cannavale

AGE RESTRICTION: PG-13

SHOWING TIMES: (MON-FRI: 6pm-8pm), (SAT-SUN: 8pm-10pm)

SYNOPSIS: Jumanji: Welcome to the Jungle is a 2017 American fantasy adventure comedy film directed by Jake Kasdan from a screenplay by Chris McKenna, Erik Sommers, Scott Rosenberg, and Jeff Pinkner. The film is the third installment in the Jumanji film series and a sequel to Jumanji (1995). The story focuses on a group of teenagers who come across Jumanji, now transformed into a video game twenty-two years after the events of the 1995 film. They find themselves trapped in the game as a set of adult avatars, seeking to complete a quest alongside another player who has been trapped since 1996.
